Introduction

The PHR system helps you to collect, track and share past and current information about your health or the health of someone in your care. Sometimes this information can save you the money and inconvenience of repeating routine medical tests. Even when routine procedures do need to be repeated, your PHR can give medical care providers more insight into your personal health story.

Normal Reference Values

Each circle shows the normal reading for diastolic Blood Pressure, Heart rate,Respiratory Rate, oral temperature, that user should try to maintain by adopting healthy life-style.

ABOUT US

Our application helps you to take an active role in monitoring your health and healthcare by creating your own PHR. Our PHRs are an inevitable and critical step in the evolution of health information management (HIM). Medical records contain information about your health compiled and maintained by each of your healthcare providers. A PHR is information about your health compiled and maintained by you. The difference is in how you use your PHR to improve the quality of your healthcare.
